Contrast Finance 2.0 — v0.40.49

Google Sheets annual statistics corrections:
- Annual statistics now highlights completed plan cells with green fill:
  - company income cells when company income >= company plan;
  - department income cells when department income >= department plan;
  - manager income cells when manager income >= personal manager plan.
- Backend annual export payload now includes department monthly plans and manager personal monthly plans.
- Empty monthly sheets are automatically hidden from the Google Sheets bottom tab bar after export.
- Non-empty monthly sheets are automatically shown again if events appear later.
- Apps Script exporter bumped to v0.6.2.
