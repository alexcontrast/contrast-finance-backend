Contrast Finance 2.0 — v0.40.51

Google Sheets export performance update:
- Payment requests are split into two sheets:
  - "Заявки на оплату": active requests (new/to pay/paid but money not in cash).
  - "Архив заявок": paid + cash received and cancelled/rejected requests.
- Apps Script no longer colors payment/money statuses row-by-row.
- Status coloring is now implemented via Google Sheets conditional formatting rules.
- Both request sheets keep the same columns, filter, widths, and header styling.
- Export payload now sends active and archive request rows separately.
- Dry-run counters now include active/archive counts and total count.
- Apps Script version: v0.6.4.
