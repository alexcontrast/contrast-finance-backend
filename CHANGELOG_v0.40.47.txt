Contrast Finance 2.0 — v0.40.47

Google Sheets archive: yearly export and daily 00:00 auto-export.

Added:
- Backend endpoint POST /google-sheets/export-year?year=YYYY&dry_run=true|false.
- Yearly payload that includes all 12 monthly archive sheets, one common payment requests sheet, and the annual statistics sheet.
- Apps Script receiver v0.6.0 with support for payload.sheets.months and payload.sheets.annual_stats.
- New sheet "Годовая статистика": yearly summary by months, manager income by months, and manager salary by months.
- Admin UI buttons in "Google архив": check/export selected month and check/export full year.
- Daily background export scheduler: runs once per day at 00:00 Asia/Qyzylorda and exports the current year.

Kept:
- Existing v0.5.9 monthly table layout and logic: agency commission row, VAT credits as income, gross/net tax logic, manager percent label, and grouped money formatting.
- Existing manual month export remains compatible.

Environment:
- GOOGLE_SHEETS_DAILY_EXPORT_ENABLED=true by default.
- GOOGLE_SHEETS_DAILY_EXPORT_HOUR=0 and GOOGLE_SHEETS_DAILY_EXPORT_MINUTE=0 by default.
- If GOOGLE_SHEETS_EXPORT_WEBHOOK_URL or DATABASE_URL is missing, the scheduler is skipped safely.
