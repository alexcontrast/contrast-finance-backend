Contrast Finance 2.0 — v0.40.48

Google Sheets archive: annual statistics corrections.

Changed:
- Monthly mini-summary and annual statistics now show VAT and taxes to pay, not gross VAT/tax before deductions.
- VAT to pay is calculated by month: client VAT minus all contractor VAT credits for the month, with floor at 0.
- Taxes to pay are calculated by month: tax only for ИП Contrast Event minus all tax deductions for the month, with floor at 0.
- OУР без НДС and Упрощенка tax expenses are not included in the summary tax-to-pay line.
- Annual statistics now includes a "Доход по отделам" section.
- Annual statistics no longer shows "Остаток до цели".
- Annual statistics "Расходы" now includes monthly expenses plus department head salaries.
- Annual statistics "Доход после расходов" was renamed to "Чистый доход" and equals the founder-share pool after expenses and department head salaries.
- Annual statistics columns were narrowed and font reduced so the year table fits on one screen more comfortably.
